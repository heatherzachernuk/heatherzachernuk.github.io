This is a prototype using literature as a basis for learning a language. It explores the first sentence of the children's novel "The Little Prince" by Antoine de Saint Exupery, from English to Chinese.

The page explores all aspects of the text for learning Chinese - a vocabulary list for each word, an audio recording for pronunciation, and the stroke order for writing each character.
