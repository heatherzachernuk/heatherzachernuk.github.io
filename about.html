Hi, I'm Heather

I am a user experience and interface designer. I can help you create or revise your website or mobile application design.

I believe that great design does more than just add visual beauty. I believe it can help solve problems and accomplish goals.

I want to help you to reach your goals and solve your user’s problems.

Here’s how I can help you:

Research

To start, I'll work with you to understand the target users of their project and the key goals of the project.

Strategy

Using that information, I will strategically organize the site/ app content throughout the pages of the site/ app. I will also plan how your users can move though and interact with your site/app.

Wireframes

Then, I will create a layout and interaction design that will allow users to move through your site/ app in the most effective way.

UI Design

Once the final layout has been decided upon I will add in colours and typography that best represent your brand.

Testing (Optional)

If your time and/or budget allows I will help you devise a testing strategy. This will allow us to check how your users understand the content and the layout design of the site/app. Based on the results I will update the design.

Testing can come before or after the UI design depending on the needs of the project.
