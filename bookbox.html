BookBox

About BookBox

Bookbox helps users to design, print and build a small box that looks like a favorite book.

The user enters details for a book - title, author, wordcount, and a favourite cover image. Then they can play around with fonts and colours to get the design to their liking. The screen shows a dynamic 3D view of the design, with the height and width of the book scaling based on the wordcount. Once the user is satisfied with the design, they can print it to a paper net to make their bookbox.

You can read about the original concept by James Bridle here.
